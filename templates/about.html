Online Voting System (Polling_App)

An online polling app is a digital platform that enables users to create, distribute, and participate in polls or surveys over the internet. These apps have gained popularity for their convenience, accessibility, and ability to gather opinions and feedback from a large audience quickly. Here are some key features and aspects commonly associated with online polling apps:

User-Friendly Interface

Online polling apps typically offer a user-friendly interface that allows both poll creators and participants to easily navigate through the platform.

Creation of Polls

Admin can create various types of polls, open-ended questions, rating scales, and more. Poll creators can customize the appearance and options of their polls

Real-Time Results

Many online polling apps provide real-time results, allowing participants to see how the poll is progressing and viewing the overall responses instantly.

Responsive Design

Many online polling apps are designed to be responsive, ensuring a seamless experience across various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
